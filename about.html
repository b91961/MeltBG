ABOUT US:

Gastro Pub with Fine Grub: Americana and Fusion Cuisine accompanied with Fine Wines and Craft Beer.

CONCEPT

The Melt Shoppe is a fusion of the past and present. The namesake pays homage to the prohibition era of “Chocolate Shoppe’s” that featured fine foods and desserts while covertly whetting the palates of the masses with bootleg spirits. Prohibition is officially over with the opening of the Melt Shoppe in historic downtown Bowling Green!

FINE GRUB

Our tantalizing menu items are aligned with carefully selected fine wines and craft beers creating a visceral dining experience. We put the “MELT” in Melt Shoppe with the inclusion of two types of Melts: cheese for sustenance and ice cream for decadence! Our staple menu allows our guests to build their own melt whether a grilled cheese, mac & cheese, or protein (burger, chicken or steak). Our dessert menu features an array of Meltdowns allowing our guests to enjoy Malt or Malt with a Twist!

JACK LIVES HERE.

The Melt Shoppe is proud of the exclusive partnership with Jack Daniels featuring signature sauces and menu items coupled with their signature whiskey. Our guests are welcome to enjoy fine dining and spirits on the venerable Old No. 7 Patio.
