  • ABOUT

    The Melt Shoppe is a fusion of the past and present. The namesake pays homage to the prohibition era of Chocolate Shoppe's that featured fine foods and desserts while covertly wetting the palates of the masses with bootleg spirits. Prohibition is officially over with the opening of the Melt Shoppe in historic downtown Bowling Green!
